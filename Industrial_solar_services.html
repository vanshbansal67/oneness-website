Solar Panel Installation Steps

Our expert team follows a simple and effective process to install commercial solar power systems.

1

Site Survey and Assessment

Our team evaluates your premises, analyzes sunlight exposure, and understands your energy requirements. We then design a custom solar power solution for you.

2

Engineering and Design

Our engineers design the most suitable solar panel system for your business, taking into account all technical specifications, permits, and regulatory requirements.

3

Permits and Approvals

We work with local authorities and utility companies to obtain all necessary permits and approvals, ensuring your project is legal and safe.

4

Installation

Our trained technicians efficiently install the solar panel system, including panels, inverters, wiring, and monitoring systems, with minimal disruption.

5

Inspection and Connection

After the system is installed, it is inspected to ensure it meets all safety and performance standards. We then connect your system to the grid.

6

System Activation and Monitoring

In the final step, we activate your solar power system and provide you with an advanced monitoring system to track its performance, allowing you to monitor your savings and production.
