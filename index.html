How long does installation take?+

For residential systems, installation typically takes 1-3 days depending on system size and complexity. Commercial and industrial installations may take longer, usually 1-4 weeks. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption to your home or business operations.

What maintenance is required for solar panels?+

Solar panels require minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning to remove dust and debris (usually 3-4 times a year) is recommended to maintain optimal efficiency. We offer annual maintenance service contracts to ensure your system performs at its best for years to come.
